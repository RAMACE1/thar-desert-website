Our Village Story

For centuries, our family has called this desert village home. Nestled between endless sand dunes, our small community has preserved the authentic ways of Marwar region life. We welcome travelers from around the world to experience genuine Indian village culture.

🏜️ Desert Heritage

Centuries of traditional desert living and Marwar culture preserved through generations

🌱 Sustainable Living

Eco-friendly practices supporting local community and desert ecosystem

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Tradition

Multi-generational family hospitality and authentic cultural experiences

🤝 Cultural Exchange

Meaningful connections between international visitors and local community
